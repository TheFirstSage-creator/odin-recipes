Chowmin Recipe

Description

Chowmin is a popular Chinese dish that has been adapted to suit local tastes in many countries.
It typically consists of stir-fried noodles, vegetables, and a choice of protein such as
chicken, beef, shrimp, or tofu. The dish is known for its savory and slightly sweet flavor,
often enhanced with soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and other seasonings.
